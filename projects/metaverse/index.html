Metaverse Townhall @ Deloitte India

I successfully organized a groundbreaking metaverse townhall within Deloitte India for the Oracle HCM practice, where I played a key role as the only female developers involved in the development process. The event featured various interactive spaces, including a "Meet Your Leaders" zone, an innovation showcase, client depictions, and a comprehensive display of Deloitte stories, highlighting the diverse hobbies of our team members. The initiative garnered significant recognition from the leadership team, underscoring the success and innovation of the event. This experience not only showcased my technical proficiency but also demonstrated my ability to contribute creatively to team projects, making it a valuable addition to my portfolio.
