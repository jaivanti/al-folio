Welcome! I am a senior undergrad student, majoring in Production Engineering at VJTI Mumbai, India. I am broadly interested in computer science, with a focus on Deep Learning and its applications. I am currently working as a research intern with Professor Ferdinando Fioretto on Video enhancing algorithms at Syracuse University. I also like working on frontend ideas and creating code designs.

Broadly speaking, lately I have been interested in working around health and Tech developing or brainstorming ideas on how we can use technologies like AI or congnitive computing to develop products which can help solve complex problems in Healthcare, etc. Since Lockdown, I have been participating in healthcare hackathons like MIT hacking medicine, MedHacks, etc, and eventually experiences and discussions from such platforms have greatly influenced me to be more curious about the ideas revolving around computer science, Human behaviour towards health and Interaction between both. Before entering Berkeley and the world of HCI, I was branded as an electrical (hardware) engineer, with a research background in [opto]electronic materials and devices and a couple of years working as a Display Electrical Engineer at Apple.

I am also curious in understanding how things around work and how our imaginations within can turn into positive realities!

In my free time, I am seeing reading about current affairs, writing blogs, designing or enjoying a cup of tea.

My research interest includes Deep learning, Computer Vision and DeepFakes Detection
